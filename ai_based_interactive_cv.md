PROJECT PROPOSAL: AI-Powered Interactive Hiring Portfolio
Executive Summary
Build a modern personal portfolio website that converts job seekers from passive resume submitters into active presenters of their expertise. Instead of competing against 400 applicants in a broken ATS system (0.4% success rate), create an interactive AI-powered interface that hiring managers discover through networking and public visibility. When they arrive, they experience conversational depth and honest fit assessment—converting casual visitors into engaged evaluators.

Problem Statement

Traditional hiring: 0.4% application success rate
Hiring managers spend 6 seconds per resume, filtering for disqualifying signals
Job seekers can't compress years of expertise into 6-second bullet points
AI-generated content has collapsed signal value of polished documents
Candidates have no control over their hiring manager's first interaction with them

Solution
A website that serves as a conversion optimization layer on top of organic discovery (networking, content, public work). When hiring managers arrive—through your LinkedIn link, networking, or visibility—they encounter an AI-powered interface that demonstrates genuine expertise through conversation, not claims.

Project Scope
Core Features
1. AI Chat Interface ("Ask AI About Me")

Conversational AI trained on your actual work, projects, and expertise
Handles multi-turn dialogue with context retention
Answers grounded in real accomplishments, not generic responses
Honest acknowledgment of knowledge gaps when appropriate
Difficulty of faking depth = credibility signal

2. Expanded Experience Context

Resume bullets paired with "View AI Context" buttons
Full story behind each achievement:

The situation/problem you inherited
Actions you took
Outcomes and lessons learned


Makes implicit expertise explicit
Example: "$1.2M savings" → reveals $4M AWS spend inherited, cost transparency built, waste identified, rightsizing executed

3. Skills Dashboard

Three-column layout: Strong | Moderate | Gaps
Honest about limitations (not just marketing strengths)
Builds credibility through self-awareness
Shows hiring manager exactly where you fit best

4. Bidirectional Fit Assessment Tool

Hiring manager pastes a job description
AI analyzes your experience against requirements
Strong fit scenario: Explains mapping with specific evidence

"You need platform infrastructure expertise—I have 6 years at Stripe and Datadog"


Weak fit scenario: Honest recommendation to not waste their time

"This needs consumer product expertise. My career is B2B. Some concepts transfer, most won't. You should keep looking."


Signals confidence, self-awareness, and mutual respect for time

5. Professional Design

Clean, modern layout with candidate branding
Name, title, company/organization logos
Clear visual hierarchy and navigation
Mobile responsive
Fast load times


How It Works: The Complete Flow
Discovery Phase (Your Responsibility)

Networking: Share your site link when talking to people
Public Content: Write about your work, learnings, projects
LinkedIn: Add site link to profile, include in applications
Community: Participate in relevant communities, conferences, forums
GitHub: Link projects back to your site

Conversion Phase (What the Site Does)

Hiring manager clicks your link from LinkedIn, networking conversation, or your content
They land on a professional portfolio
Instead of reading static bullets, they can:

Chat with AI about your expertise
Dive into the full context of your achievements
Assess their own fit against your background


They move from "filtering mode" (6-second skim) to "investigation mode" (5+ minute engagement)

Outcome

Hiring manager becomes invested in understanding you
They've verified your expertise through conversation (not credential gaming)
They've honestly assessed fit from both directions
If it's a match, they already want to talk to you
If it's not, they appreciate the directness and remember you positively
